PRINT SHOP SPECS

  • Size: 24" wide × 36" tall (2 ft × 3 ft) — standard A-frame insert
  • Resolution: 300 DPI minimum for crisp edges on the Bebas Neue type
  • Material: Coroplast (corrugated plastic) or foam core — weather resistant for outdoor use
  • Finish: Matte laminate preferred (no glare for passersby to read)
  • Bleed: Add 0.125" bleed on all edges if your print shop requires it
  • QR Code: Replace the placeholder QR with a real one generated at qr.io or qrcode-monkey.com pointing to checkmatemobiledetailing.com
  • To export: Open this file in Chrome → File → Print → "Save as PDF" → set paper to custom 24" × 36"
  • Print shops: Staples, FedEx Office, or a local sign shop (search "coroplast sign printing near me")
  • Cost estimate: ~$25–60 per side at a local sign shop; order 2 panels (front + back)
