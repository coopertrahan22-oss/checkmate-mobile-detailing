FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you want to know before you book.

How does booking work?

Pick your date and time online or give us a call. We'll confirm your location, give you a quote, and show up ready to work. No drop-off, no waiting — we come to you.

How long does a detail take?

A full interior and exterior detail typically takes 3–5 hours depending on vehicle size, condition, and pet hair. We don't rush — we stay until it's done right.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

We don't leave until you're satisfied. If something isn't right, we fix it on the spot — no questions asked. Your car should look like we just described it or we're not done.

Do you work in bad weather?

Rain affects the exterior process. If weather is a problem, we'll reach out to reschedule at no cost. Interior-only services can usually proceed regardless of weather.

How do monthly plans work?

We schedule a recurring appointment that works for you — monthly or every 6 weeks. You'll get a reminder before each visit. Cancel anytime with no fees or penalties.

What areas do you cover?

We serve all of Southeast Massachusetts and Cape Cod — from New Bedford up to Taunton, Plymouth, and across the Cape. Not sure if we reach you? Just call us.

Do you detail trucks and SUVs?

Yes. We detail everything from compact cars to full-size trucks, SUVs, and vans. Larger vehicles are quoted based on size — we'll give you a straight number before we start.

What do you need from me?

Access to your vehicle and a water source if possible (a garden hose works). We bring all our own equipment and products. Just unlock the car and let us handle the rest.
