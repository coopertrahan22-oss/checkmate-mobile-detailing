## GOOGLE REVIEW LINK
Set up your Google Business Profile and get your direct review link:
1. Go to business.google.com
2. Search your business → "Get more reviews"  
3. Copy the short link and use it in every after-service text

**Pro tip:** Save each script as a template in your phone's keyboard shortcuts  
(Settings → General → Keyboard → Text Replacement) so you can fire them in seconds.
